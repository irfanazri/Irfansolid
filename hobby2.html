Study Group

Jan 23 2025 | 16+ | 16 Episodes | Language : Korean

Directed by:Lee Jang-hoon and Yoo Beom-sang 

Written by: Eom Seon-ho and Oh Bo-hyun

Starring: Hwang Min-hyun, Han Ji-eun, Cha Woo-min, Lee Jong-hyun, Shin Su-hyun, Yoon Sang-jeong, and Gong Do-yu. 

Study Group tells the story of Yoon Ga-min, who is only talented in fighting, trying to study hard to achieve his goal of entering university by forming a study group in the notorious Yusung Technical High School.

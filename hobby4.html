Directed by:PJung Ji-hyun 

Starring: Kim Tae-ri, Nam Joo-hyuk, Kim Ji-yeon, Choi Hyun-wook and Lee Joo-myung

In 1998, Na Hee-do is a high school fencer. After her team is shut down, she moves to a new school and becomes a national athlete. Baek Yi-jin’s rich family loses everything. He works part-time jobs and later becomes a sports reporter. In the present, Hee-do’s daughter Kim Min-chae finds her mom’s old diary and learns about her past.

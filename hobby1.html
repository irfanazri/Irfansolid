Welcome to Samdal-ri

Dec 2 2023 | 18+ | 16 Episodes | Language : Korean

Directed by:Park Seon-ho 

Written by: Han Seol-hee and Hong Bo-hee

Starring: Ji Chang-wook and Shin Hye-sun.

Cho Yong-pil and Cho Sam-dal were childhood friends in Jeju. After Yong-pil’s mom dies from a wrong weather report, he becomes a weather forecaster. Sam-dal moves to Seoul to be a fashion photographer. When her life goes wrong, she returns to Jeju. She meets Yong-pil again, and they still have feelings for each other.
